People from other countries are shocked by how bad the technology is in the U.S

By JORDAN M. ELLIS

November 6, 2025

People all over the world were very worried about the health of the U.S. tech industry today after hearing that some well-known U.S. tech companies weren't doing well.People outside the U.S. were shocked by the drop because they thought U.S. IT managers would do better.This also made markets all over the world less stable.

Investors around the world sell when U.S. tech stocks go down

When the best IT stocks in the U.S. suddenly fell, investors all over the world sold their shares, which made the market drop even more.Many foreign merchants were shocked to see that companies that were once thought to be leaders in new ideas were starting to show signs of weakness.

People are beginning to worry that U.S. technology isn't as strong or important in the global markets as it used to be.

The market isn't strong when tech isn't strong

The market became much less stable when big U.S.tech stocks fell.A lot of IT companies are in the major U.S. stock indexes, so even small drops can have big effects on the economy as a whole.The market's fragility was shown by today's collapse, which caused prices to rise and investors who don't want to take risks to move to safer assets.

People are starting to question whether the U.S. is the best place for IT

Some experts think it might take a while for trust to come back because international investors aren't sure how strong the U.S. IT industry really is.Companies that are already having trouble keeping up with fast growth are even more stressed out because they are worried about slower innovation, more competition, and higher borrowing costs.

People all over the world are beginning to see things in a new way.People are now wondering if the U.S. can stay ahead in the digital world.

American tech companies keep an eye on the markets in other countries

What U.S.IT companies do in the next few weeks will be very interesting to international investors.People might feel better if businesses say they are making a lot of money, show a better outlook, or give hints of new ideas.But if businesses keep losing money, it might be easier to sell things all over the world.

The way other countries are reacting shows that how well U.S. tech stocks are doing is still an important part of how people around the world feel about the market.

JORDAN M. ELLIS

ABOUT AUTHOR

Jordan M. Ellis is an American journalist and author who writes about the people and numbers in the economy, the stock market, and other areas.
