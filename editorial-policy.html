Editorial Policy

Investment Banking is an independent online financial publication committed to factual, transparent, and accountable journalism. Our editorial standards ensure readers can trust our reporting.

Editorial Independence

We do not accept payment, gifts, or influence in exchange for coverage. All editorial decisions are made solely by our journalists and editors, free from corporate, political, or government interference. Advertising, sponsorship, and distribution partnerships do not grant any external party editorial control over our reported articles.

Accuracy and Verification

Accuracy takes priority over speed. Our journalists verify information using trustworthy sources, document-based reporting, and direct attribution. When information cannot be independently confirmed, that uncertainty is clearly stated. Errors are corrected transparently in accordance with our Corrections Policy. Stories involving criticism, allegations, legal matters, or reputationally sensitive facts use careful wording and, where appropriate, include source notes and pre-publication outreach.

Fairness and Balance

We seek diverse viewpoints on complex topics while avoiding false balance. Claims are evaluated based on evidence and credibility, not ideology.

Transparency

Readers deserve to understand how reporting decisions are made. We commit to:

  • Clearly distinguishing between news reporting, opinion, and analysis
  • Publishing articles under an individual journalist's byline whenever possible
  • Using an "Investment Banking Staff" byline for collaborative reporting
  • Labeling sponsored, paid, affiliate, or partner-funded material clearly so readers never mistake it for independent reporting

Disclosure and Labeling

Investment Banking discloses any material relationships that a reasonable reader would consider relevant to understanding a piece of coverage. Paid content, sponsored placements, affiliate links, or partner-funded material is clearly labeled and kept separate from independent reporting.

Ethical Standards

No undisclosed conflicts of interest

Our journalists disclose relevant personal or financial relationships when applicable.

No hidden sponsored content

Paid or sponsored material is always clearly labeled and separated from news coverage.

Respect for privacy

We balance the public's right to know with individual rights and personal dignity.

Reader Feedback and Accountability

Journalism improves through dialogue. Readers are encouraged to contact our editorial team with feedback, corrections, or concerns at editorial@investment-banking.org. Our commitment is to independent, fact-based journalism — accountable to readers and guided by evidence.
